Conditions for Cat Boarding

1. Cats must be vaccinated for common diseases and rabies.

2. A health check certificate from a veterinarian is required, confirming the absence of serious illnesses.

3. The owner is responsible for any damages caused by the cat, and the payment will be based on the assessed value by the boarding facility.

4. The boarding facility is not liable for any damages incurred.

Please read and understand all the conditions before availing cat boarding services.
