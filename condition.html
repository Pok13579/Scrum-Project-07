Conditions for Cat Boarding

Home Contact us Room Log in

  • The cat must be at least 4 months old.

  • Basic vaccinations must be administered, and the cat must be in good health without contagious diseases.

  • The cat must be free from fleas, ticks, ringworm, and receive regular preventive care.

  • Nail trimming and ear cleaning will be performed before boarding.

  • The owner must acknowledge that the facility is not responsible for any incident resulting in the cat's death during boarding.

  • In case of illness, veterinary services are available, but additional charges may apply.

  • If the cat is not picked up at the scheduled time, the facility will adhere to legal regulations.
